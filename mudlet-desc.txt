Mindlink: Telepathic Communication Ledger (v1.2.0)

A lightweight, zero-dependency tabbed chat capture system for Achaea. 
Mindlink cleanly organizes your channels, tells, and local interactions into a customizable Geyser interface so you never miss a message to screen spam. 

Features include native multi-line GMCP routing, mathematical main-window highlighting, flawless color-preserved emote capturing, smart ignore-lists for environmental text, and automated, timestamped roleplay logging to your local hard drive. 

To configure your tabs, layout size, or logging preferences, simply open the Mindlink script in your Script Editor and adjust the configuration block at the top. You can also type "mindlink help" in the game to see a list of on-the-fly commands and profile sharing tools!
